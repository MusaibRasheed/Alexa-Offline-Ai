
You can ask your Alexa assistant the following commands.
Remember to start with **"Alexa"**!

### 1. Basic Essentials
- **"Alexa, what is the time?"** - Tells you the dashboard time.
- **"Alexa, who are you?"** - Introduces itself.
- **"Alexa, my name is [Your Name]"** - Teaches it your name.
- **"Alexa, what is my name?"** - Recalls your name from memory.
- **"Alexa, tell me a joke"** - Tells a programming joke.

### 2. Information & Media
- **"Alexa, Wikipedia [Topic]"** - Searches Wikipedia (e.g., "Alexa, Wikipedia Python").
- **"Alexa, play [Song Name]"** - Plays a song on YouTube (e.g., "Alexa, play Believer").
- **"Alexa, open Google"** - Opens Google in your browser.
- **"Alexa, open YouTube"** - Opens YouTube in your browser.
- **"Alexa, check weather"** - Asks you for a city and tells the weather.

### 3. Productivity
- **"Alexa, remind me"** - Starts the reminder process (it will ask what to remind and when).
- **"Alexa, shutdown computer"** - **WARNING**: Shuts down your PC in 10 seconds.
- **"Alexa, stop"** or **"Alexa, exit"** - Closes the assistant.
